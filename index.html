ANNOUNCEMENT

Fansi Finance is a decentralized token under ERC-20 protocol, allows users to stake FSI, borrow assets, and vote for improvements within the community.
Fansi Finance staking platform enables investors to earn a yield on FSI token, depositing, and selecting the amount you want to stake, you will get an APR of 25%, and it can be unlocked anytime, and you will get FSI Token reward also.
Borrowers can borrow in an overcollateralized (perpetually) way, selecting USDT (Tether), Ethereum (ETH), Tron (TRX) or Bitcoin (BTC).

Yield Farming – Users will still be able to farm for the new FSI Token with their FSI Token.

Earn Connect is a lending aggregator that strives to attain the highest yield for supported coins (BTC, ETH, TRX, USDT) at all times. It does this by programatically shifting these coins between several lending protocols (AAVE, dYdX, and Compound) operating on the Ethereum blockchain.

Only 2,250,000 FSI minted and will be distributed to the community through our Airdrops and Pre-sale.
